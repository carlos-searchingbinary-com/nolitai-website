Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

The Short Version

nolit.ai processes everything on your Mac. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no way to access your data. Your meetings are yours alone.

What We Collect

Nothing. nolit.ai does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. We do not operate servers. There is no data to collect.

How nolit.ai Works

nolit.ai is a native macOS application that runs entirely on your Mac. All processing happens locally on your device:

  • Audio capture is processed by Apple's ScreenCaptureKit and Speech frameworks, on your Mac
  • Transcription uses Apple's on-device Speech framework — no audio is sent anywhere
  • AI insights are generated by machine learning models running locally on your Apple Silicon chip
  • Translation uses Apple's on-device Translation framework
  • Meeting data is stored as encrypted files in your local Application Support directory

Network Connections

nolit.ai makes no network connections during normal use. The app works completely offline.

The only network activity occurs if you choose to download optional AI models during initial setup. These downloads fetch model weights from Hugging Face (a public model repository) and do not transmit any of your data.

Data Storage

All meeting transcripts, insights, and summaries are stored locally on your Mac in:

~/Library/Application Support/NolitAI/

Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. You can delete all stored data at any time from the app's Privacy settings, or by simply deleting the folder above.

No Accounts

nolit.ai does not require you to create an account. There is no sign-up, no login, no email collection. You download the app and it works.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics frameworks, crash reporters, or tracking pixels. We do not know how many people use nolit.ai, how often they use it, or what features they use. This is intentional.

No Third-Party Services

nolit.ai does not integrate with any third-party services that could access your data. There are no SDKs, no embedded frameworks, and no APIs that phone home.

macOS Permissions

nolit.ai requests the following macOS permissions, all of which are processed entirely on-device:

  • Screen Recording — to capture system audio from meeting apps via ScreenCaptureKit
  • Microphone — optional, for capturing your own voice alongside system audio
  • Calendar — optional, to auto-detect scheduled meetings and pre-fill context
  • Reminders — optional, to export action items as tasks
  • Speech Recognition — to use Apple's on-device speech-to-text engine

None of these permissions result in data leaving your Mac. Apple's frameworks process everything locally.

Children's Privacy

nolit.ai does not collect data from anyone, including children. There is nothing to comply with because there is nothing to collect.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page. Given our architecture (no servers, no data collection), significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@nolit.ai.
