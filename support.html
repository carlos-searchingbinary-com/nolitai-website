Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant Screen Recording permission?

Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Screen Recording and enable nolit.ai. This permission allows the app to capture system audio from meeting apps. It does not record your screen — only the audio stream is used.

Why does nolit.ai need Screen Recording for audio?

On macOS, capturing system audio (what comes through your speakers) requires the Screen Recording permission via Apple's ScreenCaptureKit framework. This is an Apple platform requirement, not a design choice. nolit.ai only captures the audio stream — no screenshots or video are taken.

Does nolit.ai work offline?

Yes, completely. All transcription, AI analysis, and translation run on your Mac using locally installed models. The only time nolit.ai needs internet is during initial setup to download AI models.

How much disk space do the AI models need?

The required Meeting Intelligence model is approximately 2.5 GB. Optional models (speaker recognition, enhanced transcription, chat, visual detection) add 1-4 GB each. You choose which to download during setup.

Which Macs are supported?

nolit.ai requires a Mac with Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, M4 or later) running macOS 14 Sonoma or later. The AI models use the Neural Engine and Metal GPU, which are only available on Apple Silicon.

Which meeting apps does nolit.ai work with?

nolit.ai works with any app that produces audio: Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, FaceTime, Slack Huddles, and more. It captures system audio, so it's not limited to specific apps.

Where is my meeting data stored?

All data is stored locally on your Mac at ~/Library/Application Support/NolitAI/. Data is encrypted at rest with AES-256. You can delete all data anytime from Settings > Privacy.

How do I delete all my data?

Open nolit.ai, go to Settings > Privacy, and click "Delete All Data". This permanently removes all stored meetings, transcripts, and insights from your Mac. You can also manually delete the folder at ~/Library/Application Support/NolitAI/.

Can I export my meetings?

Yes. nolit.ai supports exporting to Apple Notes, Apple Reminders, Mail, Markdown, PDF, and JSON. Use the Export button on any meeting detail view.
